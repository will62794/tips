Super basic LaTeX template:
```latex
\documentclass[12pt]{article}

\usepackage{amsmath}
\usepackage{amssymb}

\begin{document}

\title{Template}
\author{William Schultz}
\date{\today}

\maketitle

\section{Introduction}

Hello world.

\end{document}
```
